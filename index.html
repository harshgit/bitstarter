DocSpeed provides the much awaited coordination between the Doctors, Patients and the pharmacy. This app provides realtime updates on doctor's availabilty, appointment delays and it even sends out the notification to your preferred pharmacist to get you medicines delivered to your door step. Check the app on your mobile phone, tablet or computer to get the realtime calendar of your primary physician so that you can plan well and not wait hours in the doctor's clinic.

DocSpeed also unites your doctor with your preferred pharmacist and after your clinic visit the your primary physician just has to upload your prescription and your preferred pharmacist will have it delivered to your door step. Now how cool is that?
